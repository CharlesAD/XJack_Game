Welcome to XJack

I hope you're good at mental arithmetic...

Time to beat the dealer. Your score is the total value of your cards.
The aim of the game is to get your score to 21 or as close as possible without
going over otherwise you will go BUST!!! If you're closer to 21 than the dealer
you win. If you and the dealer have the same score than it's a draw.

Boost Feature

The player can activate the Score Boost feature to add bonus points to their score
(the bonus score could be anyway from 1-5 points).

Freeze Feature Explained

The player can use the Freeze feature to pause the countdown timer
but they will gain an additional 3 points to their score.

Card Values

Ace = 11 or 1

King = 10 | Queen = 10 | Joker = 10

9 of any suit = 9 | 8 of any suit = 8 | 7 of any suit = 7 | 6 of any suit = 6

5 of any suit = 5 | 4 of any suit = 4 | 3 of any suit = 3 | 2 of any suit = 2


Rule's of the Game

1) If a player draws an ace while his current hand total is equal to 11 or greater, they will choose for their Ace card to be of value 1

2) There is only one dealer and one player

3) If the Dealer's total is 16 or less they must draw and if it is 17 or more they must stand

4) The dealer cannot "Hit" or "Stay". One the player is capable of this

5) The player can "Hit" or "Stay" as many times as they would like

6) Once you have decided the value of your Ace card you cannot change it

7) If both the Dealer and The Player hit 21 the game ends in a draw
